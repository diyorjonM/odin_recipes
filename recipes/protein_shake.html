Proetin Shake

Description

A nice peanut butter and banana protein shake to help you hit you macros

Ingredients

  • 1 Scoop of vanilla protein powder
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tbsp of peanut butter
  • 2 cups of water
  • A few cubes up ice(optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the banana into smaller pieces and put it in the blender
  2. Add the peanut butter, protein powder, milk, and ice
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Pour into a cup and enjoy
