Hot Dog

Description

A nice easy to make meal that will fill you up

Ingredients

  • Hot dog buns
  • Sausage
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Relish

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a pot
  2. Cook the sausages for 5 mins
  3. Toast the hot dog buns
  4. Put the sausage in the bun
  5. Put on whichever condiments you would like
