Mac n Cheese

Description

This crockpot mac and cheese recipe is creamy, comforting,
and takes just moments to assemble in a slow cooker. Great
for large family gatherings and to take to potluck dinners. It's always a big hit!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • Salt and blaack pepper to taste
  • 16 ozshredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 can evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 2 cups whole milk

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil and cook your pasta for 8 minutes
  2. Mix in the butter, salt, and pepper
  3. Whisk evaporated milk and eggs together
  4. Mix in the whole milk and then add it into the pasta
  5. Sprinkle the rest of the cheese on top
  6. Cook on low for 3 hours
